## Why automation agencies need a white-label dashboard

When you build AI workflows for clients using Vapi, Retell, Make, or n8n, the work is invisible. Clients can't see it running. They can't measure the ROI. And you can't charge recurring revenue for something they can't see.

A white-label dashboard changes that. It turns your backend automation into a visible, branded product your clients log in to every day — and pay for every month.

## What to look for in a white-label AI dashboard

**1. Native integration with your existing stack**

The dashboard should pull data directly from Vapi, Retell, Make, and n8n without you building custom connectors.

**2. Full white-labeling**

Your client should never see the underlying platform. Custom domains, your logo, your brand colors — all of it.

**3. Stripe billing built in**

Look for platforms with native Stripe integration so you can set up client billing without touching payment code.

**4. Real-time analytics**

Clients need to see their automations working in real time. Call volumes, workflow runs, success rates — live data builds trust and reduces churn.
